## About

I am a PhD candidate in Sustainable Development at Columbia University. My research focuses on environmental economics and applied microeconomics, with particular interest in the health and behavioral effects of environmental quality, and the economics of climate and natural resources.

Before Columbia, I was a Pre-doctoral Research Fellow at the Climate Impact Lab and the Energy Policy Institute at the University of Chicago (2019–2022), and I hold a Master of Arts in Computational Social Science (Economics Concentration) from the University of Chicago and dual Bachelor's degrees from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
