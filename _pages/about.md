## About

I am a PhD candidate in Sustainable Development at Columbia University. My research focuses on environmental economics and applied microeconomics.

Before Columbia, I was a Pre-doctoral Research Fellow at the Climate Impact Lab at the University of Chicago, and I hold a Master of Arts in Computational Social Science from the University of Chicago and dual Bachelor's degrees from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
